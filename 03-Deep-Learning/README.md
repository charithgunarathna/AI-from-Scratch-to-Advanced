Deep Learning involves complex AI systems inspired by the functionality of the human brain. It is the core technology behind advanced applications such as image recognition and speech recognition.

---

## 🧠 Neural Network Architecture

* **Input Layer:** The layer where initial data is fed into the network.
* **Hidden Layers:** The intermediate layers that process and analyze the data.
* **Output Layer:** The final layer that provides the ultimate decision or prediction.

---

## 👁️ Image Recognition (CNN)

**Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN)** are specifically utilized to detect and identify patterns, shapes, colors, and objects within an image.
