## 🤖 Large Language Models (LLMs)
These are massive AI models that have learned by reading vast amounts of data available on the internet.
* **GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer):** Used primarily for text generation.
* **Fine-Tuning:** Enhancing and adapting a pre-trained model for a highly specific subject or task.
